Phase 3 Architecture: Continuous ecosystem with pheromone fields (food & danger trails that diffuse and evaporate), a central nest storing colony resources, and continuous reproduction when colony food exceeds thresholds. Ants have 3 vision sensors + pheromone detectors + recurrent memory. The NN decides: explore, follow trails, return to nest, or flee — all emergent. 18 inputs → 10 hidden (recurrent) → 3 outputs (turn, speed, pheromone deposit).
